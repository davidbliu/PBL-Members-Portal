Setup Steps:
  1. One person visits the Cub-E located on the left side of the GBC building, if you’re facing GBC from Sproul Plaza. It’s a little nook tucked into the side of the building.
    1. You will need to provide your Cal ID to the attendant in order to check out one table, and two to four chairs.
    2. Set these up immediately in order to reserve a spot on Sproul.
  2. At least one officer and another member will need to visit the Heart Gym Cages.
    1. Enter on the side facing Hearst Annex (which looks like a Trailer Park.
      1. The entrance has two doors on the sides, and stairs if you were to go straight.
      2. Enter on the first door on the right when facing the building.
    2. Follow the hallway down. You will take a slight left turn one time, and keep walking until you see the door to the cage room (16). The door closest to the entrance is usually open, but if you end up needing the door code (keypad), refer to an officer.
    3. Find our cage (#7). It’s the one in the southwest corner (nearest the first entrance) in the central bunch, on the bottom.
    4. Retrieve the tablecloth, tent, and a very large stack of flyers.
